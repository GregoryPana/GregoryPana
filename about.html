This is Me

Here you can find out some information about me and what makes me tick.

Hi, I'm Gregory Panagary — a recent Computer Engineering graduate originally from the beautiful islands of Seychelles. After completing my degree in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, I’ve returned home and am now looking to contribute my skills locally. I'm passionate about creating smart, practical solutions at the intersection of software, hardware, and intelligence — with a strong focus on full-stack development, machine learning, computer vision, and data analytics.

During my studies, I developed a range of impactful projects, including AI-powered surveillance systems, smart agriculture platforms, and business analytics dashboards. These hands-on experiences sharpened my ability to work across the tech stack — from training deep learning models and writing backend logic to building intuitive front-end interfaces. I take pride in being a problem solver who’s both technically grounded and user-focused.

What drives me is the opportunity to use technology to solve real-world problems in ways that are ethical, inclusive, and sustainable. I’m especially interested in systems that improve decision-making, automate tasks, and elevate user experience — and I’m always eager to explore new tools that push those boundaries further.

Outside of tech, I’m an active and creative person. I enjoy digital drawing, video editing, and discovering new music, as well as gaming with friends. Being back in Seychelles, I love spending time outdoors — whether it's hiking, relaxing at the beach, or playing sports like football and table tennis. Growing up here gave me a strong appreciation for nature and balance, which continues to shape how I live and work. My multicultural experiences across different environments have helped me become adaptable, empathetic, and collaborative — qualities I carry into every team and project I’m part of.
